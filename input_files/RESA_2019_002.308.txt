EXCERPT OF THE LIMITED PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT
SIGNED ON 27 DECEMBER 2018

1. NAME

 

The name of the partnership is HERITAGE ASSETS SCSp (the “Partnership”).

2. DURATION

The Partnership has been established on 27 December 2018 for an unlimited period of time.
3. PURPOSE

The purpose of the Partnership consists in the investment in movable and immovable assets and the
holding and management of a portfolio of such movable or immovable assets.

The Partnership may manage and increase the value of its portfolio in any form Whatsoever.

In principle, the Partnership may carry out any transaction and perform all operations, linked directly or
indirectly to its purpose and Which it may deem useful for the accomplishment and development of its

purpose.

The Partnership shall not carry out any commercial or industrial activity.
